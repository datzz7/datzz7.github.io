Yamaha

Yamaha YZF-R1

The Yamaha YZF-R1, or simply R1, is a 1,000 cc-class sports motorcycle made by Yamaha. It was first released in 1998, undergoing significant updates in 2000, 2002, 2004, 2006, 2007, 2009 and 2015.

Suzuki

Suzuki GSX-R1000

The Suzuki GSX-R1000 is a supersport motorcycle made by Suzuki. It was introduced in 2001 to replace the GSX-R1100 and is powered by a liquid-cooled 999 cc inline four-cylinder, four-stroke engine although originally 988 cc from 2001 to 2004.

Kawasaki

Kawasaki H2-R

The Kawasaki Ninja H2 is a "supercharged supersport"-class motorcycle in the Ninja sports bike series manufactured by Kawasaki, featuring a variable-speed centrifugal supercharger.
